About me

Born in California, Scandinavian-bred and currently London-based. I studied design at Chelsea College of Arts, Design Management at Parsons the New School of Design, Sustainable Purchasing at Copenhagen School of Design & Technology and recently completed an MA in Design & Environment at Goldsmiths, University of London. I have a background in Design and Communications, having worked at INDEX: Design to Improve Life®, AIAYU, Designit and the Carbon Trust. A passion for sustainability and creativity has been a common thread throughout the international upbringing and various work and study experiences.
